Canvera Yougraphy - Online Printing

Canvera brings a wide range of customizable online printing solutions that can translate your beautiful personal memories and moments into creations that you will be able to preserve life-long. We have 6 categories of products that you can choose from in order to print online – Prints, Posters, Canvas, Calendars, Youbooks and Sharebooks.

Within these as well there are variants based on size, type of paper, display options, orientation, number of pages and so on. Some options are designed to capture the moments you cherish, in a print form, while the others are designed to capture a set of moments that tie together to form a journey.Some of our services are focused on giving you the pleasure of seeing a photo masterpiece you captured converted into a larger-than - life display, while others are focused on helping you keep them personal to you and your family. So, essentially, we are a digital photo lab, that combines specificity along with customizations.We offer a diverse set of specific options for you to choose from, as well as give you the ability to customize each of those options based on your needs and preferences. Our digital photo services are way too simple as well – Shoot a picture, upload it and create an impact the way you really want to – crazy, wacky, emotional, fun, nostalgic – no matter what the mood. And, the range of photos we are able to cover extend from the pocket sized ones taken for Instagram to the large high resolution ones that are taken from your DSLR. Our philosophy is - If they are fit to shoot, they are fit to put up.You can liven up your home or work cubicle.It’s #yougraphy. In order to give you a complete product, we even have a range of accessories that we offer along with our products, so that you can choose any of those such as Magnetic Ropes, Wall-safe Washi Tapes, Rope and Clips, to go with your printed photos.
